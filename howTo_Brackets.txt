================================================================================
              BRACKET 2: CONTRARIAN + HEDGE (15 min)
================================================================================

GOAL: Differentiate where public is wrong, hedge championship outcome

STEP 1: Identify Elite 8 (Contrarian Mix)
  □ Open round_probabilities.csv
  □ Apply TIER SYSTEM to pick 8 teams:

  TIER 1 (P_E8 > 60%): ALWAYS PICK - ignore ratio
    → Lock these in first

  TIER 2 (P_E8 40-60%): Pick if E8_Contrarian_Ratio < 0.6
    → Good contrarian value

  TIER 3 (P_E8 25-40%): Only if ratio < 0.4 AND you want max differentiation
    → High risk, use sparingly (0-1 team max)

  TIER 4 (P_E8 < 25%): NEVER PICK
    → Too low probability

  TARGET: 3 from Tier 1 + 4-5 from Tier 2 + 0-1 from Tier 3 = 8 teams
  VERIFY: 2 teams per region

STEP 2: Pick Different Champion (Hedge Bracket 1)
  □ If Bracket 1 = Michigan → Pick Duke
  □ If Bracket 1 = Duke → Pick Michigan or Arizona
  □ Or pick contrarian (ratio < 0.3)

STEP 3: Build Final Four
  □ From your 8 Elite 8 teams
  □ Sort by P_FF
  □ Pick top 3 + 1 contrarian (if strong ratio)

STEP 4: Fill Early Rounds
  □ Use P_R32, P_S16 columns
  □ Pick team with higher probability for each matchup
  □ Goal: Get TO your Elite 8

STEP 5: Submit

================================================================================
              CONTRARIAN RATIO EXPLAINED
================================================================================

Ratio = Public % / Model %

LOW ratio  = Public undervalues = OPPORTUNITY
HIGH ratio = Public overvalues = AVOID

Examples:
  Houston:  15.1% model, 2.2% public → 0.15 ratio (SEVERELY undervalued)
  Arizona:  27.3% model, 8.6% public → 0.31 ratio (undervalued)
  Duke:     45.4% model, 15.1% public → 0.33 ratio (slight undervalue)
  UConn:    9.7% model, 7.3% public → 0.75 ratio (fairly valued)
  Purdue:   6.1% model, 8.2% public → 1.34 ratio (OVERVALUED - fade)
